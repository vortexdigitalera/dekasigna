USB layout recommendation:

- Place the UEFI bootloader at EFI/BOOT/BOOTX64.EFI for AMD64 Intel firmware booting.
- Keep AutoSecureBootFix.nsh and UpdateKeys.nsh at the root of the image for shell-based recovery flows.
- Keep scripts and lab assets in clearly labeled folders.
- Prefer UEFI-native ISO delivery and avoid CSM-dependent legacy boot assumptions.
